What is Civic Tech?

TL;DR: The intersection of technology and public life.

A civic philosophy and a community using technology to improve public, government, and civic process and services, or to address issues of shared public concern using methods of development that are user-centred, iterative, data-driven, open sourced, and collaborative.

See our Toronto projects list and resources page for reference and more information.
